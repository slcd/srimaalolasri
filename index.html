Catering 100% Pure Veg

Delight your guests with a pure veg catering experience like no other. Our culinary experts artfully craft exquisite vegetarian dishes, using the freshest and finest ingredients. From delectable appetizers to mouthwatering main courses and indulgent desserts, our menu offers a wide array of flavors and textures that will satisfy even the most discerning palates.

Photography

Our skilled photographers expertly document the candid emotions, vibrant atmosphere, and intricate details, ensuring every significant aspect is preserved for eternity. With an eye for composition and a passion for storytelling, we turn fleeting instances into timeless keepsakes, allowing you to relive the magic of your event for years to come. Trust us to deliver captivating images that encapsulate the spirit and beauty of your celebration.

Videography

Capturing life's most memorable moments through the lens, our SLCD videography services provide professional, cinematic footage that preserves the essence and emotion of your special occasions. we skillfully document every detail, ensuring a timeless keepsake that can be cherished for years to come. Let us tell your unique story with our expert SLCD videography.

Decoration

Immerse in a world of enchantment as our meticulously crafted event stage decoration takes center. With a harmonious blend of captivating colors, elegant props & stunning lighting effects, we transport you to a realm of extraordinary beauty. Every detail is thoughtfully designed to create a visually stunning ambiance that perfectly complements the theme of your event.

Garland

Transform your special day into an enchanting celebration with our exquisite wedding garland. We create stunning, handcrafted garlands tailored to your unique style and theme, adding an elegant touch to your venue decor. Our skilled artisans use only the finest, fresh flowers and lush greenery to design garlands that radiate beauty and romance. Let us bring your wedding vision to life with our meticulous attention to detail and create an unforgettable atmosphere for you and your guests.

Thambula bags

Elevate your wedding traditions with our bespoke thambula bags. We curate personalized and beautifully crafted bags filled with thoughtful gifts and tokens of appreciation for your guests. From traditional to contemporary designs, our thambula bags reflect your unique style and add a touch of elegance to your celebrations. Trust us to create a memorable and cherished experience for your loved ones, leaving them with a lasting impression of your special day.

Mangala Isai

Enhance the auspicious atmosphere of your wedding ceremony with our captivating mangala isai. Our talented musicians and performers will serenade your guests with melodious tunes and traditional wedding songs, creating an enchanting ambiance. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage and joyous melodies that will make your wedding truly unforgettable.
